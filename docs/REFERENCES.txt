WHY THESE LIMITS & THE APPROACH

- Twelve Data free plan is commonly cited around ~8 requests/min and ~800/day (credits model).
- Token Bucket Rate Limiter is a standard, production-grade pattern used widely (e.g., Amazon SP-API).

These justify defaults:
  TD_PER_MIN ≈ 8
  TD_DAY_CAP slightly below 800 (headroom), default 760 here.
